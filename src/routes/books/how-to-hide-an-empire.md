Could you imagine China having army basees in Iceland, Liverpool and Turkey? A history of the
overseas expansion, influence and control of The United States. The US has been a brutal and
expansive governor of many territories outside its Northern American east-to-west states. This
illuminates an often ignored aspect of the US influence.
